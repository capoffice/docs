# Power outage

In case of a power outage you will lose access to the air conditioning, internet, and the entrance door will be locked.

**Open the entrance door:** If the power is not restored within a short amount of time, you need to open the door using the **office key** which is kept on Tae's desk:

![image](assets/images/officekey.png)

For safety reasons, please open the left entrance door using this key at the bottom frame. Keep the door open as long as staff remain in the office.

**Leaving the office:** If the power outage appears to continue for a long time, consider emptying the office and [lock it](pages/reception/opencloseoffice.md). 

When people leave the office, use the emergency stairs next to the men's room. The last person out locks the entrance door and notifies Tae and Kristian.

**Toilets will work:** The toilets will work for some time during a power outage.

**Power restoration:** Check that the air condition and internet is properly restored when the power returns. If the air conditioning does not push cold air, please switch it off and contact Tae or the [building technician](pages/itequipment/tehcnicalstaff.md). If the air condition is not cooling, please switch it off. If it appears that it might take a long time before air conditioning is restored, consider emptying the office.
